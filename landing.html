Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is a state in southern India known for its temples, beaches, and cuisine. Some popular tourist places in Tamil Nadu include:

  • Madurai - known for the Meenakshi Temple
  • Chennai - the capital city of Tamil Nadu
  • Coimbatore - gateway to the hill stations of Ooty and Coonoor

Karnataka

Karnataka is a state in southern India known for its heritage sites, national parks, and IT hubs. Some of the top tourist destinations in Karnataka include:

  • Bangalore - The Silicon Valley of India
  • Mysore - The City of Palaces
  • Hampi - The UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Coorg - The Scotland of India

Kerala

Kerala is a state in southern India known for its palm-lined beaches, backwaters, and network of canals. Some of the top tourist destinations in Kerala include:

  • Alleppey - The Venice of the East
  • Munnar - The Hill Station
  • Kochi - The Queen of the Arabian Sea
  • Wayanad - The Land of Paddy Fields

Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is a state in southern India known for its beaches, temples, and spicy food. Some of the top tourist destinations in Andhra Pradesh include:

  • Visakhapatnam - The Jewel of the East Coast
  • Tirupati - The Holy City
  • Konaseema - The Godavari Delta
  • Amaravati - The Capital City

Telangana

Telangana is a state in southern India known for its historic sites, scenic beauty, and delicious cuisine. Some of the top tourist destinations in Telangana include:

  • Hyderabad - The City of Pearls
  • Warangal - The Cultural Capital of Telangana
  • Nagarjuna Sagar - The World's Largest Masonry Dam
  • Charminar - The Iconic Monument of Hyderabad
